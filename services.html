Behavioral Problem-Solving

Jumping on guests. Leash reactivity. Separation anxiety. Aggression toward other dogs. These aren't character flaws — they're communication problems, and they're solvable. We'll identify what's driving the behavior and build a practical plan to change it.

Best for: Dogs with specific, frustrating behaviors that haven't responded to basic training. No judgment — these cases are my favorite ones to work on.

What's included

  • In-depth behavioral assessment (first session)
  • Root cause identification — not just symptom management

  • Owner coaching on reading your dog's signals
  • Progress benchmarks at every 2nd session

A few notes on pricing

No contracts

Pay per session or buy a package. No long-term commitments required.

Packages save money

Multi-session packages offer a discount of 10–15% versus per-session pricing.

Cancellation policy

24-hour notice required for cancellations. Life happens — just give me a heads-up.

Free discovery call

Not sure which service is right? Book a free 15-minute call — tell me about your dog and I'll recommend the best path forward. No charge, no pressure.

Equipment may be required

Some training programs work best with specific tools — an e-collar, training cot, long line, slip lead, or prong collar depending on your dog's needs and goals. I'll let you know exactly what's recommended before you purchase anything. Most items run $20–$150 and are available online or at local pet stores. Nothing is required upfront — we'll talk through it together first.
