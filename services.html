Private 1-on-1 Sessions

The most effective way to train any dog. I come to your home — where your dog actually lives — and we work through your specific challenges together. You're involved in every session, so you walk away with the skills and confidence to keep things moving between appointments.

Best for: Dogs of any age with specific behavioral goals, owners who want fast results and personalized attention, or anyone who's tried group classes and needs more.

Group Training Classes

A fun, social way to build foundational skills. Learn alongside other dog owners in a small, controlled group setting. Your dog practices listening even with distractions around — which is exactly what real life looks like.

Best for: Dogs who need socialization practice, owners who enjoy a community atmosphere, and anyone working on basic obedience in real-world conditions.

What's included

  • Small class sizes (max 6 dogs)
  • Weekly skill progression across 4–6 weeks
  • Printed training guide for each class
  • Open Q&A time each session

Puppy Foundation Training

The habits your puppy builds in the first few months become the habits they carry for life. This program focuses on building confidence, bite inhibition, leash walking, crate training, and the core commands that make everyday life easier — and more fun.

Best for: Puppies 8 weeks to 6 months. The earlier you start, the easier it is.

What's included

  • In-home sessions designed for puppies
  • Crate training & potty training guidance
  • Socialization plan tailored to your neighborhood
  • Bite inhibition & impulse control work
  • Foundation: sit, stay, come, leave it, leash walking

Behavioral Problem-Solving

Jumping on guests. Leash reactivity. Separation anxiety. Aggression toward other dogs. These aren't character flaws — they're communication problems, and they're solvable. We'll identify what's driving the behavior and build a practical plan to change it.

Best for: Dogs with specific, frustrating behaviors that haven't responded to basic training. No judgment — these cases are my favorite ones to work on.

What's included

  • In-depth behavioral assessment (first session)
  • Root cause identification — not just symptom management
  • Custom behavior modification plan
  • Owner coaching on reading your dog's signals
  • Progress benchmarks at every 2nd session
