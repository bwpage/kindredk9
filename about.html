My Story

Dogs have always been my people.

I grew up in the Salt Lake Valley surrounded by dogs — and I quickly realized that most behavior problems aren't about the dog at all. They're about communication. When dogs and their owners speak the same language, everything changes.

After years of working with dogs of every breed, size, and background, I launched Kindred K9 to bring that kind of personal, patient, relationship-first training directly to families in Davis County and the surrounding communities.

I'm not a franchise. I'm not a facility. I'm your neighbor — someone who genuinely cares about your dog and wants to see you both thrive together.

Force-free methods

Positive reinforcement because it works and because it's right.

Owner education

I teach you, not just your dog — so results last long after we're done.

No cookie-cutter plans

Every session is designed around your specific dog and home environment.

Honest about timelines

Real results take time. I'll always give you a straight answer about expectations.

My Approach

Training rooted in trust, not fear.

The methods I use are built on decades of behavioral science. But more than that — they're built on the simple belief that dogs want to work with us, not against us. Our job is to make that easy for them.

01

Read the whole dog

Behavior doesn't happen in a vacuum. I look at environment, history, and body language before I touch a training plan.

02

Train where life happens

The kitchen. The front door. The leash. Results that only work in a training facility aren't real results.

03

Keep you in the loop

I explain everything I'm doing and why, so you're not dependent on me — you're empowered on your own.
